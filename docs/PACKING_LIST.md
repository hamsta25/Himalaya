## Packing Tips

### Clothing Strategy
1. **Layer System:** Instead of bulky items, layer thin pieces
2. **Color Coordination:** Pack clothes that mix and match
3. **Wash and Repeat:** Plan to do laundry or buy local
4. **Compression:** Use compression bags for winter clothes
5. **Wear Bulkiest:** Wear heaviest shoes and jacket on flights

### Weight Management
1. **Check Limits:** Know your airline's baggage allowance
2. **Weigh Before:** Weigh luggage at home before leaving
3. **Leave Room:** For souvenirs and shopping
4. **Distribute Weight:** Balance between checked and carry-on

### Organization
1. **Packing Cubes:** Separate by category or day
2. **Rolling Method:** Roll clothes to save space
3. **Shoes First:** Pack shoes around edges, stuff with socks
4. **Fragile on Top:** Keep breakables accessible
5. **Quick Access:** Important items in outside pockets

### Smart Packing
1. **Photocopies:** Make copies of important documents
2. **Divide Valuables:** Don't put all money/cards in one place
3. **Medication Split:** Some in checked, some in carry-on
4. **Zip-Locks:** Protect electronics and liquids
5. **List in Bag:** Keep packing list in suitcase for repacking
