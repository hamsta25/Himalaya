## Important Notes

### Weather Considerations (January)
- **Karachi:** Mild winter, 15-25°C (60-77°F)
- **Punjab Cities:** Cold, 5-20°C (41-68°F)
- **Islamabad:** Cold, 2-15°C (36-59°F)
- **Himalayan Region:** Very cold, -10 to 5°C (14-41°F), possible snow
- **Tashkent:** Cold, -5 to 5°C (23-41°F)

### Packing Essentials
- Heavy winter clothing for mountain regions
- Formal wear for weddings
- Layered clothing for varying temperatures
- Comfortable walking shoes and hiking boots
- Camera and photography equipment
- Travel adapters and power banks
- Medications and first aid kit

### Documents Required
- Valid passport (6+ months validity)
- Pakistan visa (if required)
- Uzbekistan visa (if required)
- Travel insurance
- Hotel confirmations
- Flight tickets (print and digital copies)
- Emergency contact information
