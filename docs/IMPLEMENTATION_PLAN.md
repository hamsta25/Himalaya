## Phase 3: Risk Management & Contingencies

### Risk Assessment

#### High-Risk Items
1. **Weather in Mountain Region**
   - **Risk:** Road closures on KKH due to snow/landslides
   - **Mitigation:** Book flexible accommodation, have flight backup plan
   - **Contingency:** Skip mountains if roads closed, extend Islamabad/Lahore time

2. **Flight Delays/Cancellations**
   - **Risk:** Domestic flights often delayed in Pakistan
   - **Mitigation:** Book morning flights, allow buffer days
   - **Contingency:** Alternative ground transportation plans

3. **Health/Altitude Sickness**
   - **Risk:** Altitude sickness in mountain regions (8,000-10,000 ft)
   - **Mitigation:** Gradual ascent, medication (Diamox), hydration
   - **Contingency:** Descent to lower altitude, medical evacuation insurance

4. **Political/Security Situation**
   - **Risk:** Regional security concerns
   - **Mitigation:** Monitor travel advisories, local contacts
   - **Contingency:** Modify route, skip regions if unsafe

#### Medium-Risk Items
1. **Vehicle breakdown on long drives**
   - Hire reliable rental company with roadside assistance
   
2. **Lost documents/theft**
   - Keep copies in cloud storage and with trusted contact
   
3. **Food/water illness**
   - Carry medications, drink bottled water only, careful food selection

### Emergency Protocols

#### Medical Emergency
1. Contact travel insurance emergency line immediately
2. Locate nearest hospital (list in EMERGENCY_CONTACTS.md)
3. Contact embassy if serious
4. Keep all medical receipts for insurance claim

#### Lost Passport
1. Report to local police immediately
2. Contact embassy/consulate
3. Use passport copies to facilitate replacement
4. May need emergency travel document

#### Travel Disruption
1. Contact airline/hotel immediately
2. Use travel insurance if applicable
3. Modify itinerary as needed
4. Keep documentation for potential refunds
