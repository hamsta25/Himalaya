## If Something Goes Wrong

### Medical Emergency
1. Call hotel front desk for immediate assistance
2. Call insurance emergency line
3. Go to recommended hospital (see list above)
4. Contact embassy if serious

### Lost Passport
1. Report to local police (get police report)
2. Contact your embassy immediately
3. Have passport copy ready
4. Emergency travel document may be issued

### Lost Credit Cards
1. Call bank emergency number immediately
2. Call hotel to inform (in case someone returns it)
3. Have backup payment method

### Theft
1. Report to police (needed for insurance)
2. Contact insurance company
3. Contact embassy if passport stolen
4. Cancel cards if wallet stolen

### Natural Disaster/Weather
1. Follow hotel staff instructions
2. Contact embassy to register
3. Monitor local news
4. Adjust travel plans as needed

### Travel Disruption
1. Contact airline immediately
2. Check insurance coverage
3. Hotel can help with rebooking
4. Keep all receipts for insurance claim
