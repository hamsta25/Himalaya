# Hotel Recommendations - Faisalabad, Okara, DG Khan, Gilgit

## Faisalabad (January 16-17, 2026 - 1 night)

- **Location:** Canal Road
- **Amenities:** Restaurant, WiFi, parking
- **Pros:** Clean, central, good value
- **Booking:** booking.com

### Alternative: Hotel One Faisalabad
- **Price:** $50-70/night

- **Amenities:** Modern facilities, restaurant
- **Pros:** Part of reliable chain

## DG Khan (January 13-14, 2026 - 1 night)

### Recommended: Fort Munro Hotel
- **Price:** $30-50/night
- **Location:** City center
- **Amenities:** Restaurant, basic facilities
- **Pros:** Clean, safe, reasonable
- **Booking:** Direct booking or booking.com

### Note on DG Khan

## Gilgit (January 22, 2026 - 1 night)

### Recommended: Serena Hotel Gilgit ⭐ BEST
- **Price:** $70-100/night
- **Location:** Jutial, near Gilgit city
- **Amenities:** Restaurant, heating, mountain views
- **Pros:** Best hotel in Gilgit, reliable, warm in winter
- **Website:** serenahotels.com
- **Booking:** Direct or booking.com

### Budget Alternative: Hunza View Hotel
- **Price:** $35-50/night
- **Location:** Gilgit city center
- **Amenities:** Basic, heating, restaurant
- **Pros:** Affordable, central
- **Booking:** booking.com

### Winter Note for Gilgit
- Confirm heating is working
- January is very cold
- Serena Hotel most reliable for winter

## Booking Strategy Summary

### Priority Bookings (Book 6-8 weeks ahead)
1. **Karachi** - Wedding season, book early
2. **Islamabad** - Popular destination
3. **Hunza/Gilgit** - Limited winter options

### Can Book Later (2-4 weeks)
1. **Faisalabad, Okara, DG Khan** - More availability
2. **Tashkent** - Good hotel availability

### General Tips
- **Winter Consideration:** Always confirm heating for mountain/northern hotels
- **Flexibility:** Have backup options for Hunza/Gilgit (weather dependent)
- **Direct Contact:** Sometimes better rates calling directly
- **Reviews:** Read recent reviews for winter experiences
