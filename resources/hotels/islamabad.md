## Key Considerations for Islamabad

### Location for Monal Restaurant (Special Dinner)
The famous Monal Restaurant is located on Margalla Hills with panoramic city views. Hotels closest:
1. **Serena Hotel:** 15-20 minutes drive (BEST)
2. **Marriott:** 20 minutes drive
3. **F-6/F-7 area hotels:** 15-25 minutes

### Sightseeing Access
- **Faisal Mosque:** 15-30 min from major hotels
- **Pakistan Monument:** 10-20 min
- **Margalla Hills Trails:** 20-30 min
- **Daman-e-Koh Viewpoint:** 25-35 min

---

## Restaurant Recommendation

### Monal Restaurant ⭐ MUST VISIT
- **Location:** Pir Sohawa Road, Margalla Hills
- **Cuisine:** Pakistani, Continental, Chinese
- **Price Range:** $20-40 per person for special dinner
- **Specialty:** Panoramic view of Islamabad, especially stunning at sunset/night
- **Timing:** Sunset dinner recommended (6:30-7:30 PM start)
- **Reservation:** HIGHLY RECOMMENDED - Call ahead +92 51 229 9244
- **Dress Code:** Smart casual to formal
- **How to Reach:** 
  - Taxi/Uber from hotel: $8-15 one way
  - Hotel can arrange car: $25-40 round trip with wait time
- **Pro Tip:** Arrive 30 minutes before sunset for best lighting and photos
