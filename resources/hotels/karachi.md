## Booking Tips

### Timing
- **Book Now:** Hotels fill up quickly during wedding season (winter months)
- **Minimum Stay:** Some hotels may require 2-3 night minimum in peak season
- **Flexible Cancellation:** Request flexible rates given wedding schedules

### Wedding Season Considerations
- January is peak wedding season in Pakistan
- Hotels near popular wedding venues book out early
- Consider booking hotels that offer:
  - Late checkout (weddings often run late)
  - Early breakfast (for preparation time)
  - Laundry/pressing services for formal wear
  - Transportation coordination

### What to Ask When Booking
1. "Can you arrange transportation to wedding venues?"
2. "Is late checkout available?" (Weddings often go until 1-2 AM)
3. "Do you have laundry/pressing services for formal wear?"
4. "Is breakfast timing flexible?" (May need early or late breakfast)
5. "What are your security arrangements?" (Important in Karachi)

## Location Guide

### Key Areas in Karachi

**Clifton/Defence (DHA):**
- Upscale residential and commercial area
- Many wedding venues located here
- Hotels: Avari Towers, Beach Luxury
- Safe, well-developed area

**Saddar:**
- Central, historic area
- Budget to mid-range hotels
- Good transportation links
- More traffic, older area

**Shahra-e-Faisal:**
- Main artery connecting airport to city
- Several business hotels
- Convenient for travel days
- Hotels: Ramada Plaza, Nishat

### Distance to Key Locations
- **Jinnah International Airport:** 
  - Avari Towers: 25-30 minutes
  - Clifton area: 20-30 minutes
  - Saddar: 15-20 minutes
  
- **Popular Wedding Venues (Clifton/DHA):**
  - From Avari Towers: 10-20 minutes
  - From Clifton hotels: 5-15 minutes
  
- **Shopping Areas:**
  - Dolmen Mall Clifton: 15-20 min from Avari
  - Saddar Bazaar: 10-15 min from central hotels

## Transportation from Hotel

### Airport Pickup
- **Hotel Arranged:** $15-25 (most reliable)
- **Taxi/Uber:** $10-15
- **Careem:** $8-12 (local ride-hailing app)

### Wedding Venue Transportation
- **Hotel Concierge:** Can arrange dedicated car ($30-50 for evening)
- **Uber/Careem:** $5-15 per trip depending on venue
- **Consider:** Arranging round-trip with hotel (driver waits or returns for pickup)

### Local Getting Around
- **Uber/Careem:** Most convenient and safe
- **Hotel Car Service:** More expensive but reliable
- **Local Taxi:** Negotiate fare beforehand
