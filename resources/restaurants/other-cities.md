# Restaurant Recommendations - Karachi, Islamabad, Tashkent

## Karachi Restaurants

### Traditional Pakistani

#### Kolachi Restaurant ⭐
- **Location:** Do Darya, near beach
- **Cuisine:** Pakistani BBQ and traditional
- **Price:** $10-20 per person
- **Specialty:** Seafood, BBQ, outdoor seating by water
- **Best Time:** Dinner, especially sunset

#### BBQ Tonight
- **Location:** Multiple locations (Boat Basin popular)
- **Cuisine:** Pakistani BBQ
- **Price:** $8-15 per person
- **Specialty:** Wide variety of grilled meats, karahi
- **Note:** Very popular, expect crowds

### International

#### Cafe Flo
- **Location:** Zamzama, DHA
- **Cuisine:** French bistro
- **Price:** $15-25 per person
- **Why:** Break from spicy Pakistani food, elegant

#### The Patio
- **Location:** Boat Basin
- **Cuisine:** Continental, steaks
- **Price:** $12-22 per person

## Islamabad Restaurants

### Must-Visit

#### Monal Restaurant ⭐ SPECIAL DINNER
- **Location:** Pir Sohawa Road, Margalla Hills
- **Cuisine:** Pakistani, Continental, Chinese
- **Price:** $20-40 per person for special dinner
- **Specialty:** PANORAMIC city view (best in Islamabad)
- **Timing:** Sunset dinner (6:30-7:30 PM start)
- **Reservation:** ESSENTIAL - Call +92 51 229 9244
- **Dress:** Smart casual to formal
- **Transport:** Taxi $8-15, hotel car $25-40 round trip
- **Pro Tip:** Arrive 30 min before sunset for photos

## Tashkent Restaurants (Uzbekistan)

### Traditional Uzbek

#### Sim Sim Restaurant ⭐ BEST UZBEK
- **Location:** Near Chorsu Bazaar
- **Cuisine:** Traditional Uzbek
- **Price:** $15-30 per person
- **Must Try:** 
  - Plov (national dish - rice with meat)
  - Shashlik (grilled meat skewers)
  - Somsa (meat pastries)
  - Lagman (hand-pulled noodles)
- **Atmosphere:** Traditional Uzbek decor

- **Location:** Osh Markazi, near Chorsu
- **Cuisine:** Uzbek Plov specialist
- **Price:** $8-15 per person

## Dining Tips

### Pakistan
- **Spice Level:** Very spicy - always ask for "mild" or "medium"
- **Portions:** HUGE - order less than you think
- **Timing:** Dinner often 8-10 PM
- **Tipping:** 10% if service not included
- **Water:** Bottled only
- **Ramadan:** Check dates (affects restaurant hours if coincides)

### Uzbekistan
- **Tea Culture:** Green tea with every meal
- **Bread:** Never waste - culturally important
- **Plov Timing:** Best at lunch
- **Alcohol:** Available (unlike Pakistan)
- **Tipping:** 10% appreciated
- **Language:** Learn "Rahmat" (thank you)
