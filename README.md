## ğŸ“ Cultural Tips

### Dress Code
- **Modest clothing** required (cover shoulders and knees)
- **Women:** Head scarf for mosques
- **Formal wear** for weddings (Shalwar Kameez or suits)

### Etiquette
- Remove shoes before entering homes and mosques
- Use right hand for eating and greeting
- Ask permission before photographing people
- Respect prayer times

### Food Safety
- Drink bottled water only
- Avoid raw vegetables at street stalls
- Eat where locals eat (high turnover = fresh)
- Lahori food is very spicy - order "mild" or "medium"
